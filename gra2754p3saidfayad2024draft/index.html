Making an Octopus shown by an Octopus

1. Fold and unfold.

2. Blintz folds the corners into the center.

3. Collapse into preliminary base.

4. Fold and unfold. Repeat behind.

5. Inside reverse fold. Repeat behind

6. Open out the points, repeat from behind.

7.Blintzed bird base. Inside reverse fold the outer flaps down. Repeat behind.

8. Valley fold into the center at the same time spread to narrow the front flap. The dotted line shows the crease on the back of the spread sink.

9. Like so. Repeat step 8 behind.

10. Fold and unfold.

11. Fold the edge to the center line. Unfold.

12. Fold the edge to the crease made in step 11. Unfold.

13. Inside reverse fold starting from the innermost crease, pleating in and out to narrow the flaps.

14. Like so repeat steps 10-12 on each of the other flaps.

15. Step 13 completed. Swing one layer to the left, repeat behind.

16. Fold in as near to the center as you can. Repeat behind.

17. Swing the top layer back to the right, repeat behind.

18. Fold and unfold

19. Tuck underneath the layer below. Push in near the top of the model to fold on the crease made in step 18.

20. Fold up the top flap as far as it will go.

21. Rabbit ear.

22. Fold the flap back down.

23. Repeat steps 22-24 behind

24. Like so.

25. Close up. Fold across at the same time opening the eye a little. The fold will curve slightly.

26. Like so. Repeat on the left hand side.

27. Inside reverse fold the remaining 6 tentacles up. The position of each is up to you.

28. Fold and unfold.

29. Make the model 3 dimensional by opening out the head and blow to inflate a little

30. Finally shape the tentacles as you choose. Use a cocktail stick or bamboo skewer to make tight curls.
